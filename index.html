Joe P. Samples

Joe P. Samples is an accomplished researcher with a Ph.D. in Neuroscience and extensive experience in cell and structural biology. He has a strong background in neurobiology, respiratory biology, and cell and molecular biology, with a focus on learning and memory, cell adhesion, and cancer biology. Joe has been recognized with multiple honors and awards, including the Minority Access to Research Careers Trainee and the Society for the Advancement of Chicanos and Native Americans in Science Graduate Poster Competition Winner.
